Stay Safe Online Checklist
--

- Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts

- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible

- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown attachments

- Verify messages or calls claiming to be from banks or officials

- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions

- Keep your apps and devices updated regularly


UPI / Financial Safety Tips
--

- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone—even bank officials

- Do not approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps

- Double-check receiver details before sending money

- Avoid scanning random QR codes from untrusted sources
