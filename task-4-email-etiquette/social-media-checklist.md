Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students
--

Do’s
--

 - Think before posting anything online
 
 - Maintain a professional and respectful tone
 
 - Protect your personal information and privacy
 
 - Verify information before sharing
 
 - Use social media to learn, network, and grow

Don’ts
--

 - Do not share fake or unverified news
 
 - Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
 
 - Do not overshare personal details
 
 - Avoid engaging in online arguments or negativity
 
 - Do not post content that can harm your reputation
